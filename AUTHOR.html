Author Information

Name: Shaira Salasain Canete

University: Western Mindanao State University

Program: Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

About the Author

Shaira Salasain Canete is a first-year Civil Engineering student at Western Mindanao State University. She is passionate about promoting responsible and sustainable construction practices. With a growing awareness of the environmental impacts of cement production, particularly its contribution to carbon emissions, she advocates for eco-friendly materials, innovative technologies, and ethical engineering practices.

She believes that engineers play a vital role in shaping a sustainable future and aims to contribute to infrastructure development that balances strength, safety, cost-efficiency, and environmental responsibility for present and future generations.
