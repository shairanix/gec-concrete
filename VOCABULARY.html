Vocabulary Page

Responsible and Sustainable Use of Concrete

Vocabulary

Essential Absolutely necessary or extremely important.
Backbone The main support or foundation of something.
Infrastructure Basic physical systems of a place, such as roads, bridges, and buildings.
Durability The ability to last a long time without breaking or wearing out.
Versatility The ability to be used in many different ways.
Resilient Able to recover quickly from difficulties or damage.
Indispensable So important that it cannot be replaced.
Sustainable Able to continue long-term without harming the environment.
Carbon emissions The release of carbon dioxide (CO₂) into the atmosphere, often from burning fuel or manufacturing.
Longevity Long life or long-lasting quality.
Conserve To protect and carefully use resources so they are not wasted.
